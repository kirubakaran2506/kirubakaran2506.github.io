Restaurant Rating Prediction

Predict customer ratings from restaurant data using regression models — Decision Tree vs Linear Regression.

Problem

Restaurants have no data-driven way to understand what drives their aggregate customer ratings.

Solution

Trained Linear Regression and Decision Tree Regression on 9,000+ records — votes, location, cuisine type, and cost as input features.

Impact

Decision Tree achieved R² = 0.87 vs Linear Regression at R² = 0.61. Votes was confirmed as the strongest predictor of customer rating.
